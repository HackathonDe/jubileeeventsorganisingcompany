Our Story

Jubilee Events Organising Company Was registered in Kenya in the year 2012, it's a premium event management Company headquartered in Nairobi, Kenya. We specialize in full-service Events Consultation, management/Planning and supply of all events needs for all types of events , from weddings, Dowry, graduations, funerals, Birthdays, Corporate Events, Political events, Religious Events etc.

Founded with a mission to bring elegance and excellence to every occasion, our team combines years of expertise in design, coordination, and execution.
